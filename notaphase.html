SUN RA ARKESTRA

Welcome to the official section of "NOT A PHASE", a captivating piece of art by Jrafalet. This artwork is a visual representation of the journey of transformation and self-discovery, symbolized by the imagery of a skull surrounded by nature. In this website, you will learn more about the inspiration, meaning, and technique behind this stunning creation.

"SUN RA ARKESTRA" is a visual representation of Sun Ra's musical and philosophical vision, which transcended the boundaries of time, space, and culture. The artwork features a golden figure at the center, dressed in Egyptian attire and holding a planet in his hands. The figure symbolizes Sun Ra himself, who claimed to be from Saturn and adopted an Egyptian persona. The planet represents his cosmic consciousness and his belief in music as a universal language.

The background of "SUN RA ARKESTRA" is filled with celestial bodies, such as stars, planets, and comets, creating a sense of wonder and awe. The artist used vibrant colors and geometric patterns to create a realistic and captivating effect. The celestial bodies also symbolize Sun Ra's musical influences and innovations, which ranged from swing and bebop to electronic and experimental sounds.

The base of "SUN RA ARKESTRA" is surrounded by pyramids and sphinxes, which evoke the ancient Egyptian culture and mythology that fascinated Sun Ra. The artist used rich colors and intricate details to bring these elements to life. The pyramids and sphinxes also symbolize Sun Ra's quest for knowledge and wisdom, as well as his connection to the past and the future.

"SUN RA ARKESTRA" is a remarkable artwork that captures the essence of Sun Ra's music and philosophy, which challenged the norms and conventions of his era and inspired generations of artists and listeners. It invites viewers to immerse themselves in a world where music is not just heard but felt, where space is not just a place but a state of mind, and where ancient and modern coexist in harmony. It is an artwork that celebrates the genius and legacy of Sun Ra, the cosmic jazz master.
